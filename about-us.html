Who we are

BIGLIFT TRUCKERS LIMITED is an 100% indigenous company duly registered by the corporate affairs commission (CAC) under the Company and Alliance Matters Act of 1990 with RC. 689496. Our area of specialization include but not limited to Equipment Leasing, Engineering Services, Haulage, Clearing and Forwarding and Total Logistical Support.

BIGLIFT is poised to deliver Quality service, safely and professionally thereby creating a Good Business Relationship with numerous clients. We have outstanding zero man hour lost time both on equipment breakdown and on the site.
